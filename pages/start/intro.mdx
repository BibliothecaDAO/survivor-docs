# Intro

Loot Survivor has 2 main game modes for players to choose from.

1. **Testnet** - Instant transactions, no VRF wait times between between levels. Not eligible for $LORDS rewards for top three places or collectible beasts.
2. **Mainnet** - Average of $5 per game ($3 fixed $LORDS, $1 Gas, $1 VRF). Full bot protection. Eligible for $LORDS rewards and collectible beasts
