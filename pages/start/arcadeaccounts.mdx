# Arcade Accounts

Arcade Accounts are "burner" accounts that can interacts, with certain permissions, in a contracts.

The permissions given to the arcade account can only do limited actions for security, these include, interacting with the game functions (attack, flee, explore etc.) and transfers to a "master" account.
The "master" account is the account that created the Arcade Account (AA). It works by using native storing the keys in the browser.

Arcade Accounts are **free** to create however it will initiate a transfer of 0.01 ETH from your connected wallet to the AA to cover your transaction costs (gas) from normal gameplay.
