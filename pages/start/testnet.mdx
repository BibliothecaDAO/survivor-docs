# Getting Started On Testnet

> Testnet Loot Survivor is the perfect place to practice and hone your skills without spending any LORDS or ETH on gas before playing on Mainnet. 

- Free to play
- No player rewards
- No collectible NFTs
- Psuedo-randomness
- Leaderboard may be periodically reset

___

Getting started on Testnet is as easy as just clicking "Play on Testnet" in the intro. (A browser wallet is deployed with keys stored in the background)
