# Getting Started On Mainnet

> This is the mainnet version of Loot Survivor. It costs 25 LORDS per game (or a daily game using a Golden Token) and requires ETH for gas fees. If you are starting out we highly suggest you practice on [testnet](/start/testnet) for free.

- On mainnet
- Free to play
- 25 LORDS per game
- Full bot protection: wait to start
- Eligible for tournament prizes, LORDS rewards and collectible beasts

___

Step 1: Get a Starknet Wallet. Download [ArgentX](https://www.argent.xyz/argent-x/) or [Braavos](https://braavos.app/).

Step 2: Ensure you have at least 25 LORDS and 0.01 ETH in your wallet. 
- [Bridge LORDS from ETH to Starknet](https://realms.world/bridge) or [Buy LORDS on Starknet](https://app.avnu.fi/en?tokenFrom=0x49d36570d4e46f48e99674bd3fcc84644ddd6b96f7c741b1562b82f9e004dc7&tokenTo=0x124aeb495b947201f5fac96fd1138e326ad86195b98df6dec9009158a533b49&amount=0.001)
- [Bridge ETH to Starknet](https://starkgate.starknet.io/)

Step 3: Ensure your Starknet wallet is on mainnet

Step 4: Head to [Loot Survivor (mainnet)](https://survivor.realms.world/) 

Step 5: Connect your wallet

Step 6: Create an [Arcade Account](/start/arcadeaccounts)
- Initiates a transfer of 0.01 ETH from your connected account to cover gas from gameplay
- Initiates three contract approvals

> **Do not refresh or close the browser while these transactions are processing as it could lead to the loss of your funds**

Step 7: Connect your new Arcade Account

Step 8: Choose your weapon and name your Adventurer

Step 9: Press 'PLAY WITH LORDS TOKENS' which will spend 25 LORDS

Step 10: Wait for the countdown for your game to begin (~5 but varies mins)

> **We recommend setting a timer on your phone to ensure you don't miss your game and lose your 25 LORDS**

Step 11: Play
