## And so it begins!

To start a game you are required to choose a starting weapon. Don't worry about the choice as the first beast will always be a one hit kill.

Enter a unique name for your adventurer, this will be used to identify you on the leaderboard.

## Pay game cost

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center' }}>
    <img src="/tutorial/pay.png" alt="Start Game" width="100%" height="150" />
</div>

Here you will need to pay the cost of the game, a dynamic LORDS amount based on a fixed $3.

If you have a Golden Token in your account you can also play for free every 24 hours.
