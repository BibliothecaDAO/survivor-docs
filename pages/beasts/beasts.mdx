# Beasts

- There are 75 beasts in Loot Survivor.
- Beasts attack the Head, Chest, Hands, Waist and Feet.
- Beast have dynamic health and attack location.
- Greatness 20 beasts unlock special name prefixes.

## Base Damage Calculation

Beast base attack and armor of an item is:
`Greatness \* (6 - Tier)`
