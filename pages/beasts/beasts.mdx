## Base Damage Calculation

Beast base attack damage is:
`Greatness * (6 - Tier)`

### Beast Attack

There are 3 Beast types; Magical, Hunter and Brute. Each with their suited weapon and armor.

| Beast     | Weapon   | Armor |
