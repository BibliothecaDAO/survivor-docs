# Arcade Accounts

Arcade Accounts are "burner" accounts that can only interact with the permissions. The permissions allow the account to do limited things for security these include interacting with the game functions and transfers to a "master" account. The "master" account is the account that create the Arcade Account (AA). It works by storing the keys in the browser.
Arcade Accounts ae free to create however, it will initiate a transfer of 0.001 ETH from your connected wallet to the AA to cover your transaction costs (gas) from normal gameplay.
